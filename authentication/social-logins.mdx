## How Social Logins Work

### First-Time Social Login

When you sign in with a social provider for the first time:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Provider">
    Click on your preferred social login button (Google or GitHub)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provider Authentication">
    You'll be redirected to the provider's login page
  </Step>
  <Step title="Grant Permissions">
    Approve the requested permissions for Opsmatic
  </Step>
  <Step title="Account Creation">
    Your Opsmatic account is automatically created using your social profile
  </Step>
  <Step title="Profile Sync">
    Basic information (name, email, avatar) is synced to your profile
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Returning Users

For subsequent logins:
- Click your social provider button
- You'll be automatically signed in if already authenticated with the provider
- No additional permissions required

## Google Login

### What Information We Access

When signing in with Google, Opsmatic requests access to:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Basic Profile Information">
    - Full name
    - Email address
    - Profile picture
    - Google account ID (for linking)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Email Address">
    - Primary email for account identification
    - Used for notifications and account recovery
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Google Account Requirements

- Active Google account
- Verified email address
- Third-party app permissions enabled

<Warning>
If you're using a Google Workspace account, your administrator may need to approve third-party applications.
</Warning>

## GitHub Login

### What Information We Access

When signing in with GitHub, Opsmatic requests:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Public Profile">
    - GitHub username
    - Display name
    - Profile picture
    - Public email address
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Email Addresses">
    - Primary email address
    - Verified email addresses
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### GitHub Account Requirements

- Active GitHub account (free or paid)
- At least one verified email address
- OAuth applications enabled

## Managing Social Connections

### Viewing Connected Accounts

To see which social providers are connected to your account:

1. Go to **Profile Settings**
2. Click the **Security** tab
3. View **Connected Accounts** section

### Disconnecting Social Providers

<Warning>
Be careful when disconnecting social providers. Ensure you have alternative login methods configured.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Security Settings">
    Navigate to Profile Settings > Security
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Connected Account">
    Locate the social provider you want to disconnect
  </Step>
  <Step title="Disconnect">
    Click **Disconnect** next to the provider
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Action">
    Confirm the disconnection in the popup dialog
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Adding Additional Social Providers

To connect additional social providers to your existing account:

1. Go to **Profile Settings > Security**
2. Click **Connect** next to available providers
3. Authenticate with the social provider
4. Grant necessary permissions

## Troubleshooting Social Logins

### Common Issues and Solutions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="'Permission Denied' Error">
    **Causes:**
    - Third-party apps blocked in your social account settings
    - Corporate/organizational restrictions
    
    **Solutions:**
    - Enable third-party applications in your Google/GitHub settings
    - Contact your IT administrator if using organizational accounts
    - Try using a different browser or incognito mode
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Email Already in Use">
    **Causes:**
    - An Opsmatic account already exists with this email
    - Email used with different authentication method
    
    **Solutions:**
    - Try signing in with your existing authentication method
    - Use account recovery if you forgot your password
    - Contact support to merge accounts if needed
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Login Redirect Issues">
    **Causes:**
    - Browser blocking redirects
    - Pop-up blockers interfering
    - Cached authentication state
    
    **Solutions:**
    - Allow pop-ups for opsmatic.com
    - Clear browser cache and cookies
    - Try a different browser
    - Disable browser extensions temporarily
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Provider-Specific Troubleshooting

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Google Issues">
    **Google Account Suspended:**
    - Ensure your Google account is in good standing
    - Check Google Account status page
    
    **Workspace Restrictions:**
    - Contact your Google Workspace admin
    - Request approval for Opsmatic OAuth application
    
    **Two-Factor Authentication:**
    - Complete 2FA with Google before attempting Opsmatic login
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="GitHub Issues">
    **Private Email Settings:**
    - Ensure you have at least one public or verified email
    - Check GitHub email settings
    
    **OAuth App Access:**
    - Review authorized OAuth applications in GitHub settings
    - Ensure Opsmatic hasn't been revoked
    
    **Organization Restrictions:**
    - Check if your GitHub organization blocks third-party access
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
