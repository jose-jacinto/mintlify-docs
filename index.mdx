## What is Opsmatic?

Opsmatic is a comprehensive monitoring and analytics platform designed specifically for automation workflows. Whether you're running complex n8n workflows or sophisticated Make.com scenarios, Opsmatic provides real-time insights, performance monitoring, and health tracking to ensure your automations run smoothly.

## Key Features

### **Comprehensive Workflow Tracking**
Monitor execution status, duration, success rates, and error patterns across all your automation platforms. Get detailed insights into node-level performance and data processing volumes.

### **Advanced Analytics Dashboard**
Access real-time metrics, historical trends, and performance comparisons. Visualize your automation health with intuitive charts and customizable reporting periods.

### **Platform Availability Monitoring**
Ensure your n8n instances and Make.com scenarios are always accessible with continuous availability monitoring and uptime tracking.

### **Organization Management**
Create teams, invite members, and manage access permissions. Perfect for agencies, enterprises, and teams managing multiple automation environments.

### **Smart Alerting** *(Coming Soon)*
Get notified when workflows fail, performance degrades, or platforms experience downtime. Set custom thresholds and notification preferences.

### **Performance Optimization**
Identify slow-running workflows, resource-intensive processes, and optimization opportunities to improve your automation efficiency.
